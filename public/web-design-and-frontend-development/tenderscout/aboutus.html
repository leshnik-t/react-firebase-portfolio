About us

TenderScout started in early of 2012 and we´ve had an amazing journey so far. From simple beginnings as a public procurement services company to launching our first ´intelligent sales´ solution in 2013, we´ve focussed relentlessly on taking the guesswork out of sales. That enables our community and subscribers can focus on doing what they do best. We are humbled by the faith placed in us by our community in supporting us on this adventure and fully intend to repay that trust as we continue our incredible adventure.

Tony Corrigan

CEO & Co-Founder

Tony is the co-founder and CEO at TenderScout. He drives the company's vision, strategy and growth as it provides fresh and insightful perspective on winning competitive sales to it’s professional services community.
