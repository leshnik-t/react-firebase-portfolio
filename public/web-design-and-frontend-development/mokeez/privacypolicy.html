Privacy policy

This policy constitutes the complete agreement between you and Galaximatic Ltd. regarding the publicly accessible parts of our website. By using our website, you agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

This policy enters into force on May 25, 2018.

In this Privacy Policy, Galaximatic Ltd., "we" or "us" means

II. What personal data we collect

The type and amount of information we collect and the way we use it depends on the reason why you provide it to us.

When you contact us by phone, mail, in person or online, we may collect information about you (referred to in this Privacy Policy as "e;personal information"e;). It may include your name, ID / Passport number, personal identification number, email address, telephone number, date of birth, position, photos (when applying for work or attending events organized by us) and other personal information, which you may decide to provide us with.

Sensitive data

According to the legislation in the field of data protection, certain types of personal information are more sensitive. These are the so-called "e;sensitive"e; or "e;special categories"e; of personal data that include information revealing racial or ethnic origin, religious or philosophical beliefs and political opinions, trade union membership, genetic or biometric data, health or sexual health information, or sexual orientation of a person. Sensitive information shall only be collected when needed. In the event that we collect and process such information, we will provide you with clear notices, indicating what information is required and why.

We strongly believe in protecting the privacy of children's personal data. In line with this belief, we deliberately do not collect or process personal information for individuals under the age of 14years old, and no part of the website is targeted to individuals under the age of 14 years. III. How we collect information for you

We collect information in the following ways:

  • When you provide it to us DIRECTLY

It is possible to provide us with your personal data for the purpose of signing a service contract or to communicate with us.

  • When you authorize OTHER ORGANIZATIONS to share it with us or it is publicly available
  • When we collecting it in your use of our WEBSITE or APPLICATIONS

Cookies

Like most websites, we use "cookies" that help us to make our site - and the way you use it - better. "Cookies" are small text files that are downloaded and stored on your device when you visit a website. "Cookies" are widely used by website owners to provide you with a good internet experience and also to provide information that can help them to improve the websites.

Our website uses "cookies" with the following purpose:

  • In order our website to function
  • To collect anonymous data on how our users use our site, which is helping us to improve it
  • To allow you to share the content on social networks
  • To help us to provide appropriate advertising to those who may be interested in it

We do not use "cookies" to:

  • Collect personal information to identify a person
  • Provide personal information to third parties

Most internet browsers automatically accept "cookies" unless you change your browser settings. If you want to restrict, block, or erase cookies set by websites, you can usually do so through your browser settings. These settings are usually found in the "options" or "favorites" menu of your internet browser. Please note that if you set your Internet browser preferences to block all "cookies", you may not have access to all or part of our website. Unless you have not adjusted your internet browser settings to block "cookies", our site will use "cookies".

V. Legal basis for processing

According to the legislation in the field of personal data protection, it should be a "e;legal basis"e; for the use of personal information. The legal grounds are set out in the General Regulation on the Personal Data Protection (Regulation 2016/679) and the relevant national data protection legislation.

Specific consent

There is consent when we ask you whether we can use your information for a specific purpose and you agree with it.

Legal obligation

We have a legal basis to use your information when processing is necessary to comply with a statutory or regulatory obligation.

Performing a contract / taking steps under your pre-contractual relationship request

We have reasons to use your personal information when we are in the process of entering into a contract with you or performing a contractual obligation. For example, when you use any of our services or apply to work with us.

Vital interests

We may use your personal information where the processing is necessary to protect the interests of life and health. For example, if there is a case where we need to contact someone unexpectedly or share his or her information with Emergency Service.

Legitimate interests

We have reason to use your personal information when it is reasonably necessary for us (or a third party) to do so in our (or a third party) "legitimate interest" (provided that the purpose for which the information is used is fair and does not have an unjustified impact on your rights). We believe that our legitimate interests include all the day-to-day activities that Galaximatic Ltd. performs with personal information.

We rely on legitimate interests only when we believe that any potential impact on you (positive and negative), its degree of privacy impact, and your rights under personal data protection laws have no precedence over our (third party) interest in using your information in this way.

When we use sensitive personal information, we require an additional legal basis in accordance with the legislation on the personal data protection, so we will do on the grounds of your explicit consent or other means at our disposal for the use of this type of information (for example, if you have made it publicly available, if we have to process it for employment, social security or social protection purposes, for your vital interests or, in some cases, if it is of public interest in doing so).

VI. How we protect the security of your information

We apply technical and organizational measures to ensure that your personal information is protected. For example, our online contact forms are always encrypted and our network is protected and routinely monitored. We limit access to information on a "e;need-to-know"e; basis and undertake appropriate measures to ensure that our team members are aware that such information is used only in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We regularly review who has access to the information that we have, in order to guarantee that your information is only available to suitably trained staff and contractors.

VII. How long your information is stored

Galaximatic Ltd. applies specific criteria to determine how long your information will be stored, specified on the basis of legal and operational considerations, taking into account the guidelines of the Commission for Personal Data Protection. The principle is that we store it for a period no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

We store your collected personal information, when we have an ongoing legitimate business need for that (for example, to provide you with a service you have requested or to comply with the applicable legal, tax or accounting requirements). When we do not have a legitimate business need to process your personal information, we will erase or anonymize it, and if this is not possible (for example, because your personal information has been stored in backups), then we will securely store your personal information and will isolate it from any other processing until erasure become possible.

We will only use your information for the purposes for which we have collected it. We will only share your data for the following purposes:
