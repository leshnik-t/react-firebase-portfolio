How long does the TenderScout free trial last?

You can use the trial version, for 30 days after which time you’ll be conacted and asked if you wish to select a plan.

How does the trial work?

You'll get full access to all features of the Professional during the trial. There are no hidden costs or setup fees and no credit card required during the trial.

Can I change plans any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade whenever you want. You will be billed only for the month that's running.

Do I need a credit card for the trial?

No, we will contact you before the trial expires to see if you are benefiting from and wish to continue using TenderScout.

How do I know how many licences I need ?

You will typically want one licence for each Sales (Development) Rep involved in lead generation/qualification
