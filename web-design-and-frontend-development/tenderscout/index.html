Discover, Qualify, Enrich

Over 1,000 new leads and 750 contract award notices are added to our repository each day. Our algorithms sift through over 2,500,000 public tenders from all over the world to build pipelines of current and future opportunities.

Each lead is qualified using predictive analytics to determine the probability that the buyer would award the contract to you or to an alternative supplier.

Each lead is enriched with competitive intelligence such as the level of competition, whom the main competitors are, how the buying decision will be made and how well positioned you are to succeed.
